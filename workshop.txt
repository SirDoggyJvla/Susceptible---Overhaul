title=Susceptible - Overhaul
description=[h1]What does this mod do ?[/h1]
description=The goal of this mod is to update [url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=2795677303]Susceptible[/url] from Spyjack by adding new mod compatibility and be used as a framework by other modders to have filters and oxygen tanks be consumed by features they chose to add such as [url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=3196616278]Hazardous Zones[/url] or [url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=3128144245]Cordyceps Spore Zones[/url] via my Overhaul mods.
description=
description=[h3]Features:[/h3]
description=[olist]
description=[*]Adds [b]more mod compatibilities[/b] to Susceptible. (such as respirators from STALKER armor pack or gasmasks from Undead Survivor)
description=
description=[*][b]Permanent UI of Susceptible[/b] even without the trait. (won't show you're in danger from zombies if you don't have the trait)
description=
description=[*]Ability to [b]remove the Susceptible trait[/b] from character creation with a secondary mod ! 
description=
description=[*][b]Framework[/b] for other mods to use the [b]durability system[/b] of the original mod and the [b]list of protection types[/b] from Susceptible.
description=
description=[*]Adds [b]new functions for modders[/b] to check whenever the player has a gas mask or hazmat on with effective protection. (Other types of protections weren't added because I had no need but I can add them for modders who are interested)
description=[/olist]
