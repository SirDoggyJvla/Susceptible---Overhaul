description=[h1]What does this mod do ?[/h1]
description=The goal of this mod is to update [url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=2795677303]Susceptible[/url] from Spyjack by adding new mod compatibility and be used as a framework by other modders to have filters and oxygen tanks be consumed by features they chose to add such as Hazardous Zones or Cordyceps Spore Zones via my Overhaul mods.
description=
description=[u][b]Features:[/b][/u]
description=[olist]
description=[*]Uses the [b]list of masks and hazmat suits from Susceptible[/b] for more mod compatibilities. (such as respirators from STALKER armor pack or gasmasks from Undead Survivor)
description=
description=[*]Ability to [b]remove the Susceptible trait[/b] from character creation with a secondary mod ! 
description=
description=[*]Framework for other mods to use the durability system of the original mod.
description=[/olist]
